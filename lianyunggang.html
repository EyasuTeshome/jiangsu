Kongwang Mountain

There is an interesting story about this mountain that Confucius had once climbed to this mountain and looking over the sea. When he came here he was ready to spread the “ritual music” to the people. This place has ancient inscriptions and it’s has a group of Buddhist statues here which are the earliest Buddhist statues in the late eastern han dynasty.

Huaguoshan

Huaguoshan is the highest mountain in Jiangsu province and well known as Sun Wukong's birthplace also. If you are a lover of Magic Monkey you deserve a visit. The scenery is beautiful and big. It has over 100 scenic spots and has many statues based on its heroes.
