Get to Know Nantong

Enjoy Your Time

Located in the southeast of Jiangsu province
Nantong is known as the Pearl of the River and Sea which is also a major port on the Yangtze River near Shanghai.
It was historically known for its distinctive textiles and the birthplace of China's modern industry.

Hao River Scenic Area

Considered as one of the best preserved moat in China, the river boasts a history of more than a thousand years and with a total length strtching up to 10 kilometres. The marvelous watercourses makes the Hao river honored as an Emerald Necklace of Nantong City.

Langshan Scenic Area

Langshan Scenic Area is a popular attraction near Nantong City which is located nearby the Yangtze river. It consists of other five hills which are surrounded by water. Langshan consists of five peaks known as Five Hills, all stretched out along the east bank of the Yangtze River Langshan (Wolf Hill), Huangnishan (Yellow Hill), Jianshan (Sword Hill), Junshan (Military Hill), and Ma'anshan (Saddle Hill). The famous most splendid hill among the five hills is langshan. The Wolf Hill is also famous as Buddhist hill which has plenty of buddhist activities. Langshan was the ancient Pagoda was built during Yang dynasty when Buddhism flourished in China around year 669. May not be as magnificent as some other famous Buddhist temples but the view from the top is mind boggling. Yangtze river view, City view or the park surrounding the hill all are worth the small climb.
