Visit world’s greatest circular city wall

The City Wall of Nanjing

Located in the capital of Jiangsu, Nanjing City Wall is one of the oldest and historical remains of Ming Dynasty. It's one of China's most underrated tourist attractions. If you plan to come to Nanjing this is one of the must see place and easier to get to other nearby tourist attractions.

Nearby attractions

• Xuanwu lake

• Jiming si Temple

To get to the Nanjing city wall you can use the metro and bus.

From Nanjing south railway station located in center for metro line 1, metro line 3, metro line s1, metro line s3


Then take

• Metro line 1 to Zhonghuanmen or Xuanwumen

• Metro line 3 or Metro line 4 to Jimingsi Station, Exit 4.


If you wish to take Bus from anywhere to Nanjing

• Take bus 2, 16, 49, 63, 202, 302, 701, 706, D18, or G5 to Zhonghuamen Chengbao.

• Take bus 1, 25, 28, 30, 33, 35, 47, or 56 to Xuanwuhu Gongyuan.

• Take bus 10, 13, 22, 45, 66, 69, 72, 73, 76, 315, 318, 501, 510, 555, or 557 to Zhongyangmen Dong


Opening hour 09:00 to 17:00 from Monday to Saturday.

The ticket fare is CNY30/person.
