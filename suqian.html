Qianlong Temporary Palace

This is a well preserved old building in the south bank of the Luoma Lake, which is among the four largest freshwater lakes of Jiangsu and surrounded by scenic spots and historical sites. The temporary palace was first built around 1684, and the Emperor Qianlong of the Qing Dynasty had been to there three times for worship.

King Xiang Hometown

King Xiang is definitely the most important historical figure from this city. This is a well-maintained ancient town, with historical buildings and a statue of Xiang Yu mounted on a horse. if you are anywhere in jiangsu looking for a traditional chinese town with an amazing scenery it’s this place.
