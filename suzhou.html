Shantang Street

This is one of the most iconic sites of Suzhou. The construction of the Shantang canal to connect between Huqiu and the city. it’s a good place to see rivers, canals and the old well conserved buildings around. Specially at night when all the lights are turned on, the place looks magical. The place is good to stroll at night or at a day time looking at the ancient town. Also Suzhou is known as “Venice of china ” and this street is one of the reasons that Suzhou is seen as the “Venice of china” .

Humble Administrator's Garden

One of the largest and famous classical garden to visit and also a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The garden itself is quite huge and is impressive itself how the designers made excellent use of nature to design such an elegant garden that blends itself into the environment easily. It’s a very calming space with gorgeous buildings and a variety of pools.

Tiger Hill

This scenery is a cultural symbol of Suzhou and rich in history. There’s a large park with an interesting leaning tower at the top, and some good views. The hill is surrounded by waterfalls and gardens. While Climbing it, you can find some sites some of which can be traced back over 2,500 years to the founding of Suzhou.

Jinji Lake

This place is the largest city lake park in china located in the center of Suzhou industrial park. Nearby there is the oriental arc building also known as the gate to the east. Usually at night there is a musical fountain in the lake park that brightly lit up with neon and led lights. If you think about going to shopping there are plenty of options in this area.
