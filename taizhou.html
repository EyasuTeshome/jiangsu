Qinhu National Wetland Park

It’s the first national wetland park in Jiangsu Province and the second of its kind in China. Qintong Boat Festival, Wetland Ecological Tourism Festival and Qinhu Lake (Eight Fresh) Food Festival are held every year in the park. Qinhu is also called Magpie Lake. as its pristine environment attracted many magpies here. The black and white magpies are considered an auspicious bird by Chinese. There is a museum which highlights the wildlife and culture of the place. There are wood boats available to visitors to have a look which is the best way to see the beautiful scenery of the wetland.

Mei Lanfang Memorial Museum

It was established in memory of Mr. Mei Lanfang a famous Peking Opera artist. In his 50 years of stage career, he maintained strong and consistent performance but never avoided working on new techniques. The museum is a combination of exhibition hall for the historical materials of Mei Lanfang and the Mei Lanfang Garden to commemorate this excellent art master of Beijing Opera. The museum contains old buildings of Ming and Qing dynasties. There are good displays which are in English as well as Chinese.

Shangfang Temple

The magnificent temple jungle is a temple floating in the water. This temple is the largest water Buddhist resort in China that rebuilt in modern time. There are Broad waters, primitive ecology, diverse biological species, and rich regional religions, historical and cultural connotations have become unique development resources here.

Qianduo Scenic Area

It’s an important agricultural and cultural heritage in the world. It’s famous for it’s unique landform which was formed 750 years ago. this scenic area is crisscrossed by rivers and dotted with raised fields. The raised fields in water town have different approaches but equally satisfactory results with terraced fields on mountain.
