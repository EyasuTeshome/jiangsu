ABOUT XUZHOU

Have a Great Stay

The beautiful Xuzhou is the largest city of northern Jiangsu with its long and rich history.
It is the center of a rich coalfield, with mines in the immediate vicinity.
Xuzhou, with its long and rich history, has been designated by the national government as one of China’s historical and cultural cities.

TOURIST ATTRACTIONS

Xuzhou museum of Terra-cotta warriors

The Museum of the Han Terracotta Warriors is located on the west side of Lion Hill and displays thousands of terracotta warriors. All of the warriors have a different facial expression and different gestures. The Museum of the Han Terracotta Warriors consists of two different pits that are the locations for these warriors. In 2005 the Museum of the Han Terracotta Warriors was extended. Now it is welcoming visitors from around the world with the unique charm of the Han Dynasty (206BC-220AD). Compared with the Xian Terracotta Warriors and Horses of the Qin Dynasty, Han tomb figures are smaller but the development of their features is based on that in the Qin Dynasty. In the Han Dynasty, craftsmen paid careful attention to detail. They focused on the soldiers' inner world and their spirit. The Museum of Han terracotta warriors is the must-see travel site.
